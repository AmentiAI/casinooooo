What is Rakeback?

Rakeback originated in online poker, where the "rake" is the percentage of each pot taken by the cardroom as a fee. Rakeback programs return a portion of that fee to players. In casino gaming, the concept evolved to mean returning a percentage of your total wagered amount — regardless of wins or losses.
