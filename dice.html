How Crypto Dice Works

  1. The game rolls a number between 0.00 and 99.99.
  2. You set a target (e.g. 50) and pick roll over or roll under.
  3. Your win chance — and therefore payout multiplier — updates live.
  4. Win: payout = bet × multiplier. Lose: the bet is taken.

Example: roll-under 50 means a 49.5% win chance at ~1.98× payout. Roll-under 10 means ~9.9% win chance at ~9.9× payout.

Dice Strategies

  • Flat betting: same bet size, same target — the safest and the fairest reflection of the 99% RTP.
  • Martingale: double the bet after each loss at ~2× payout. Recovers losses on a win but explodes on losing streaks.
  • Reverse Martingale: double on wins, reset on loss. Low drawdown but capped upside.
  • D'Alembert: increase bet by 1 unit on loss, decrease by 1 on win — a gentler ramp than Martingale.

No strategy changes the house edge. What strategy does change is variance. At Winna, 60% rakeback turns the ~1% edge into a near-break-even game for flat betting.
