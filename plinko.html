How Plinko Works

  1. Pick risk level — Low, Medium, or High.
  2. Pick number of rows — typically 8 to 16. More rows = more volatility.
  3. Set your bet amount and drop the ball.
  4. It bounces randomly through pegs and lands in a slot with a multiplier from ~0.3× (center) up to 1,000× (outer edges, high risk, 16 rows).

Plinko Risk Levels Compared
